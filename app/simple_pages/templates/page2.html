Whats is Heroku and what to expect from it?

If you are looking for a great and reliable cloud based PaaS - Heroku is the answer!

Its super easy to navigate around its GUI and the best part its FREE

Its perfect for managing and deploying modern apps today

You do not need to manage infrastructure of the entire website, Heroku doe it for you

Today, Heroku operating with over than 60 billion requests and counting

Heroku interestingly enough is a business unit of Salesforce

Currently Heroku has over 13 millions applications deployed around the World!

Heroku was found back in 2007 by Kames Lindenbaum, Adam Wiggins and Orion Henry

By October 2009 Heroku already had 35,000+ applications on its platform

In December 2010 is when Salesforce purchased Heroku

Great news for Heroku, Node.js was incorporated into Heroku by June 2011

In September 2012 is when a dashboard was introduced into Heroku.

By May 2015 Heroku become friendly with Docker

Another great feature was introduced into Heroku,Heroku Shield,which supports high compliance apps
